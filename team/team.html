AUTOLAB EV TEAM

Engineering a Competitive
Electric Formula Team

Autolab EV Team is a student-led electric formula team focused on system-level engineering, data-driven development, and continuous iteration.

TIMELINE

Team History

2022

Team Founded

Autolab EV Team was established with the goal of developing a competitive Formula SAE Electric vehicle from the ground up.

2023

First Electric Prototype

Completion of the first rolling EV chassis and initial powertrain validation through track testing.

2024

System Optimization Phase

Focus shifted toward weight reduction, aerodynamic development, and reliability-oriented design iteration.

2025

Full Aero & Data-Driven Development

Introduction of full aerodynamic package and integrated data acquisition strategy for performance-based decision making.

STRUCTURE

Organization

Vehicle Dynamics

Suspension geometry, steering system, tire analysis, and on-track handling optimization.

Powertrain

Motor, inverter, drivetrain design, torque delivery optimization, and efficiency analysis.

Electronics & Control

HV/LV architecture, ECU logic, data logging, sensor integration, and system safety.

Aerodynamics

Downforce development, CFD validation, aerodynamic balance, and cooling airflow management.

Manufacturing

Composite layup, machining, welding, assembly processes, and quality control.

Management & Operations

Project planning, sponsorship relations, cost management, and competition logistics.
